About Me

My very first job was a lineman for an internet company. Then i shifted to into printing, photography, videography, and sound system. And lately I tried freelancing and virtual assistance. Even now while i write this article, I am currently enrolled in a full stack web dev bootcamp! At first glance, it may sound like i've been shifting into totally different careers, but if you look closely you'll find that all these jobs have something in common - technology and creatives. This is what Mondi Graphic Design is all about. It is the culmination of all my experiences, centered on these two common denominator
